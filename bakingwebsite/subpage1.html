4-Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

Peanut Butter Cookies have never tasted so good in the shortest amount of time! This 4 ingredient recipe takes 30 minutes or less and is gluten free!

Ingredients-

• 1 cup of Peanut Butter

• 1 cup of Butter

• 1 Egg

• 1 teaspoon of Vanilla Extract

Recipe-

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Combine all ingredients together in a mixing bowl. Then scoop dough in to balls and place on a baking pan with parchment paper. With a fork press down lightly onto the cookies. Then bake for 10 minutes, let cool, and enjoy
